About BASE64IMAGE

Free, privacy-focused Base64 image tools for developers and designers

Our Mission

BASE64IMAGE provides free, fast, and privacy-focused tools for working with Base64-encoded images. Our mission is simple: offer developers and designers reliable tools that respect their privacy while making Base64 operations quick and easy.

Why We Built This

As developers ourselves, we frequently needed to encode and decode Base64 images. Most online tools either required uploading sensitive data to unknown servers or were cluttered with ads and tracking scripts. We wanted something better—a clean, fast, privacy-respecting alternative.

That's why we built BASE64IMAGE to run entirely in your browser. No uploads, no tracking, no data collection. Just simple tools that do exactly what they're supposed to do.

Privacy-First Approach

Privacy isn't just a feature for us—it's fundamental to how we designed BASE64IMAGE. Here's what makes us different:

  • Client-Side Processing: All tools run in your browser using JavaScript. Your data never leaves your device.
  • No Data Collection: We don't collect, store, or transmit your images or Base64 strings.
  • No Tracking: No analytics, no cookies, no third-party tracking scripts.
  • No Accounts Required: Use all tools immediately without signing up or logging in.
  • Open and Transparent: Our approach is straightforward—what you see is what you get.

How It Works

When you use our tools, everything happens locally in your browser:

  • Images are read using the FileReader API
  • Base64 encoding and decoding use native browser functions
  • Image previews are generated using Blob URLs
  • Downloads create temporary links in your browser's memory

No network requests are made to our servers or any third party (except for loading the page itself). Your data stays on your device from start to finish.

Our Tools

We currently offer four essential Base64 tools:

  • Base64 Image Decoder: Decode Base64 strings to images
  • Base64 Image Encoder: Convert images to Base64 strings
  • Base64 to PNG Converter: Convert Base64 to PNG format
  • Base64 Validator: Validate and verify Base64 strings

Technology Stack

We believe in keeping things simple and fast. BASE64IMAGE is built with:

  • Static HTML: No complex server-side processing
  • Pure CSS: System fonts and minimal styling for fast load times
  • Alpine.js: Lightweight JavaScript framework for interactive features
  • Native Browser APIs: FileReader, Canvas, Blob for core functionality

This minimal tech stack ensures fast page loads, reliable performance, and complete transparency about what code is running in your browser.
