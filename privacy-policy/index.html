Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

BASE64IMAGE is committed to protecting your privacy. Unlike many online tools, we process all data entirely in your browser using JavaScript. This means your images, Base64 strings, and any other data you work with never leave your device.

No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Images you upload or decode
  • Base64 strings you paste or generate
  • File names or metadata
  • Personal information
  • Usage data or analytics
  • IP addresses
  • Browser information

How Our Tools Work

All BASE64IMAGE tools run entirely in your web browser using client-side JavaScript. When you upload an image or paste a Base64 string:

  • Your browser reads and processes the data locally
  • No network requests are made to our servers or any third party
  • All processing happens in your device's memory
  • Results are displayed instantly without any data leaving your device

No Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. The only external resource loaded is the Alpine.js library from a CDN (for tools that use it), which is a lightweight JavaScript framework that runs entirely in your browser and does not transmit any data.

No Cookies or Local Storage

We do not use cookies, local storage, or any other browser storage mechanisms to track you or store your data. Each session is completely isolated, and when you close your browser, nothing remains.

Server Logs

Our web server may collect standard server logs for security and performance monitoring purposes. These logs may include:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Date and time of access
  • Pages visited

However, these logs do not contain any of your images, Base64 data, or uploaded content. They are used solely for maintaining the security and availability of the website.

Security

Because all processing happens in your browser and no data is transmitted to our servers, your data is as secure as your own device. We recommend:

  • Using HTTPS (which we enforce) to prevent man-in-the-middle attacks
  • Keeping your browser updated to the latest version
  • Using trusted networks when working with sensitive data

Children's Privacy

Our website does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because we do not collect or store any data, we cannot inadvertently collect information from children.

Your Rights

Because we do not collect, store, or process any personal data, there is no data for you to access, modify, or delete. You maintain complete control over your data by virtue of it never leaving your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through our About page.
